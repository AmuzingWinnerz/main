FAQ

Is there a one time donation option?

Yes there is. To donate one time, you can buy bits and use bits in the chat. This is a great way to support the channel one time!

Is there a free way to sub and still support you?

Yes there is! If you have a amazon prime account, then you get one free prime sub a month, which you can use towards the channel for free and you still support us.

How do I redeem my perks?

To redeem your perks, join the discord and make sure to link your twitch account to your discord account. Then you may @Jimshaderzc to tell him that you subbed. Then you will receive your perks.

Can I donate through Paypal instead?

No, at this time we only allow donations through twitch. You can use Paypal as a payment method with twitch.
